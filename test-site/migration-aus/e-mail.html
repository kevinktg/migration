A Roadmap to Nomination

Phase 1: Portfolio Development (Months 1-3)

Sit for English tests, collate documents, and build the 'Strategic Evidence Portfolio' including case studies and concept proposals.

Phase 2: Skills Assessment (Months 4-7)

Lodge skills assessment applications with VETASSESS for both the Marketing Specialist and Graphic Designer.

Phase 3: EOI & ROI Submission (Month 8)

Submit Expression of Interest (EOI) in SkillSelect, followed immediately by a high-quality Registration of Interest (ROI) to Victoria.

Phase 4: Invitation & Lodgement (Month 9+)

Await invitation from the Victorian Government and lodge the formal Subclass 491 visa application within the 60-day timeframe.
